\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
It is a landscape descriptor. 

Contains a logical grid of tiles. Required for loading data into the grid. \begin{DoxyWarning}{Warning}
Checks do not provide full verification of the card. There may be breakdowns due to the continuity of the road 

The size is 10 by 10 tiles, but this can be changed, Tiles must be 64 by 64 pixels 
\end{DoxyWarning}
